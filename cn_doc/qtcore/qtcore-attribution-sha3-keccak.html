Secure Hash Algorithm SHA-3 - Keccak

SHA-3, originally known as Keccak, is a cryptographic hash function.

Used in Qt Core (QCryptographicHash).

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-32-rvk.macros, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-32.macros, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-64.macros, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-interface.h, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-opt32.c, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-opt64.c, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakF-1600-unrolling.macros, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakNISTInterface.c, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakNISTInterface.h, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakSponge.c, and tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/sha3/KeccakSponge.h.
