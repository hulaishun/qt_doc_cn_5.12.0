详细描述

The QGraphicsSceneMoveEvent class provides events for widget moving in the graphics view framework.

A QGraphicsWidget sends itself a QGraphicsSceneMoveEvent immediately when its local position changes. The delivery is implemented as part of QGraphicsItem::itemChange().

It's similar to QMoveEvent, but its positions, oldPos() 和 newPos(), use QPointF instead of QPoint.

参见 QGraphicsItem::setPos(), QGraphicsItem::ItemPositionChange, 和 QGraphicsItem::ItemPositionHasChanged.
