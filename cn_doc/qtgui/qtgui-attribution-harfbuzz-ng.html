HarfBuzz-NG

HarfBuzz is an OpenType text shaping engine.

Optionally used in Qt GUI. Configure with -system-harfbuzz to force the use of the system library, or -qt-harfbuzz to link statically to the library that is bundled with your Qt version.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/harfbuzz-ng.
