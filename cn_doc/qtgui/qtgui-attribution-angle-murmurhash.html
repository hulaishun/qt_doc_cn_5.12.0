ANGLE: Murmurhash

Used on Windows to implement OpenGL ES on top of DirectX. Configure with -no-opengl, or -opengl desktop to exclude.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/angle/src/third_party/murmurhash.
